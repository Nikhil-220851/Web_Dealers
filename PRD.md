# Loan Management System (LMS)

## 1. Project Title
Loan Management System – Web Application for Online Loan Application and Management

---

## 2. Problem Statement

In traditional banking systems, loan applications are handled manually. Borrowers must visit the bank, fill out physical forms, and wait for manual verification and approval. This process is time-consuming, inefficient, and prone to human errors.

The Loan Management System (LMS) aims to digitize the loan application process. Borrowers can apply for loans online, track their loan status, calculate EMI, and make payments. Administrators can review loan requests, approve or reject applications, and generate reports.

---

## 3. Objectives

The main objectives of this system are:

- Provide an online platform for borrowers to apply for loans.
- Allow users to track the status of their loan applications.
- Provide EMI calculation for borrowers before applying for loans.
- Allow administrators to manage and review loan applications.
- Maintain digital records of loans and payments.
- Generate reports for administrators.
