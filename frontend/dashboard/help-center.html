Frequently Asked Questions
How do I apply for a new loan?
Go to the 'Apply for Loans' section, select your preferred bank and product, and follow the simple 4-step process.
What documents are required for KYC?
Commonly required documents include PAN card, Aadhaar card, and recent bank statements.
How long does it take for loan approval?
Most personal and car loans are approved within 24-48 business hours after document verification.
Can I prepay my loan?
Yes, technical terms vary by bank, but most allow prepayment after a lock-in period of 6 months.
